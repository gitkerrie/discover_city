Classic food city · Gansu

Lanzhou

Clear broth, white radish, red chili, green herbs: one noodle bowl wakes the northwest.

Lanzhou tables revolve around beef, lamb, handmade wheat dishes, and clean aromatic seasoning. Beef noodles prize clear broth and precise colors, while cold niangpi and warming lamb soup reveal two complementary rhythms of northwestern eating.

What to eat in Lanzhou

  1. 01

    Lanzhou Beef Noodles

    Clear beef broth, hand-pulled noodles, white radish, chili oil, and herbs form a precise bowl.

  2. 02

    Niangpi

    Steamed wheat sheets and gluten are dressed with vinegar, garlic water, and chili oil.

  3. 03

    Hand-Grabbed Lamb

    Simply boiled lamb stays sweet and tender, served with salt, pepper, or raw garlic.

  4. 04

    Lamb Vermicelli Soup

    Clear lamb broth and slippery noodles finish brightly with pepper and cilantro.

Eat like a local
