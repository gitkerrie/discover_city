Classic food city · Chongqing

Chongqing

Beef tallow bubbles and Sichuan pepper wakes the senses; this mountain city never whispers.

Chongqing builds its food on numbing heat, but hot pot is only the beginning. Noodles, river dishes, and street snacks all lead with aroma. Sichuan pepper, chilies, and beef tallow keep flavors vivid across the city's steep streets.

Numbing intensity Rich beef tallow Streetwise spirit
01

What to eat in Chongqing

  1. 01

    Chongqing Hot Pot

    A rich beef-tallow broth for quick-cooking tripe, duck intestine, and vegetables.

  2. 02

    Chongqing Xiaomian

    Noodles layered with chili oil, Sichuan pepper, preserved greens, and peanuts.

  3. 03

    Chili Chicken

    Crisp morsels of chicken hidden among a fragrant heap of dried chilies.

  4. 04

    Hot and Sour Noodles

    Chewy sweet-potato noodles with vinegar, chili, soybeans, and pickled mustard.

02

Eat like a local

Start hot pot at a moderate heat level; garlic and sesame oil in the dipping bowl help soften the burn.
