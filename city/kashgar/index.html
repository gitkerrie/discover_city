Hidden-gem food city · Xinjiang

Kashgar

Tandoor smoke and warm spice meet the bright fruit of the western oases.

Kashgar meals revolve around wheat, lamb, and seasonal fruit. The tandoor lends food a smoky crust, cumin and onion frame the meat, and carrots or raisins bring a gentle sweetness to rice pilaf.
