Hidden-gem food city · Yunnan

Kunming

Flowers, rice noodles, and a steam pot make the Spring City taste fresh and wonderfully untamed.

Kunming is an inviting gateway to Yunnan food. Rice noodles appear in countless forms, a clay steam pot condenses its own pure chicken broth, and grilled erkuai or rose cakes turn rice and highland flowers into everyday snacks.

Many rice noodles Highland freshness Floral pastries

What to eat in Kunming

  1. 01

    Crossing-the-Bridge Rice Noodles

    Scalding broth arrives with raw and cooked ingredients to add in sequence before the noodles.

  2. 02

    Steam-Pot Chicken

    Steam condenses through a clay pot’s center to produce an exceptionally clear chicken broth.

  3. 03

    Grilled Erkuai

    A pliable rice cake is grilled, spread with sauce, and wrapped around crisp dough or vegetables.

  4. 04

    Yunnan Flower Cake

    Flaky pastry holds fragrant rose-petal filling with a light, floral sweetness.
