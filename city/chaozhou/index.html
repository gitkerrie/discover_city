Hidden-gem food city · Guangdong

Chaozhou

Pure flavors and aromatic braises meet rice-cake craft and glowing charcoal.

Chaozhou cooking respects natural flavor while extending it through master stock, fish sauce, and rice-based specialties. Beef is sliced by cut, seafood is served at peak freshness, and humble rice batter becomes a remarkable range of snacks.

Pure freshness Rice-cake craft Master-stock aroma

What to eat in Chaozhou

  1. 01

    Fresh Beef Hot Pot

    Precisely cut beef is swished by the second and paired with nutty satay sauce.

  2. 02

    Oyster Omelet

    Sweet oysters and starch batter form crisp edges, brightened with fish sauce.

  3. 03

    Master-Stock Goose

    Aromatic soy braise permeates the goose, with liver and web prized separately.

  4. 04

    Red Peach Rice Cake

    A peach-shaped rice wrapper encloses sticky rice and savory fillings.
