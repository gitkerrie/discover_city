Classic food city · Hubei

Wuhan

The city wakes over breakfast, then moves through sesame, river fish, and restorative soups.

Wuhan begins the day with guozao, its celebrated breakfast ritual. Sesame paste, rice batter, sticky rice, and fish broth fill the morning; later meals turn toward river produce and slow soups. The flavors are honest, sustaining, and full of the city's brisk energy.

What to eat in Wuhan

  4. 04

    Pork Rib and Lotus Root Soup

    Powdery lotus root and tender ribs create a gentle Hubei classic.
