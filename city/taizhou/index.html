Hidden-gem food city · Zhejiang

Taizhou

Between mountain and sea, chewy rice snacks share the table with vivid seafood.

Taizhou faces the sea with mountains at its back, combining lively harbor seafood with a broad repertoire of rice and wheat snacks. The cooking is fresh and gently savory-sweet, shaped by ginger, fermented rice, and the daily catch.
