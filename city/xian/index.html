Classic food city · Shaanxi

Xi'an

Wheat, slow-cooked meat, and warm spices build layer upon layer through the old city.

Xi'an is a food city built on wheat. Flatbreads, noodles, rich broths, and beef or lamb form its backbone. Substantial textures and bright northwestern spices make every meal feel generous and deeply satisfying.

Wheat traditions Beef and lamb Bold and hearty
01

What to eat in Xi'an

  1. 01

    Roujiamo

    A crisp, tender flatbread packed with juicy, long-braised pork.

  2. 02

    Lamb Paomo

    Hand-torn bread soaked in lamb broth and served with pickled garlic and chili.

  3. 03

    Oil-Splashed Noodles

    Hot oil blooms chili, scallion, and garlic over broad, springy noodles.

  4. 04

    Gourd Chicken

    Chicken that is boiled, steamed, then fried for crisp skin and tender meat.

02

Eat like a local

Portions are generous, so share with companions if you want to sample several wheat dishes in one day.
