Start with the flavor you crave, then connect two or three cities instead of trying to taste the whole country at once. This map gives you a simple first route.
01 · Sichuan

Chengdu

Red chili oil, sweet heat, and a city that turns every meal into a daily ritual.

Zhong Shui Jiao · Dan Dan Noodles · Long Chao Shou · Mapo Tofu
02 · Shaanxi

Xi'an

Wheat, slow-cooked meat, and warm spices build layer upon layer through the old city.

Roujiamo · Lamb Paomo · Oil-Splashed Noodles · Liangpi
03 · Guangdong

Guangzhou

Dim sum taken slowly, where freshness and exacting technique define everyday Lingnan cooking.

Shrimp Dumplings · Char Siu Bao · Roast Goose · Sampan Congee
04 · Hunan

Changsha

Fresh chilies, tangy ferments, and fierce wok heat grow livelier as the night deepens.

Stinky Tofu · Spicy Crayfish · Chopped-Chili Fish Head · Sugar-Oil Rice Cakes
05 · Hubei

Wuhan

The city wakes over breakfast, then moves through sesame, river fish, and restorative soups.

Hot Dry Noodles · Three-Delicacy Doupi · Fish Broth Rice Noodles · Pork Rib and Lotus Root Soup
06 · Chongqing

Chongqing

Beef tallow bubbles and Sichuan pepper wakes the senses; this mountain city never whispers.

Chongqing Hot Pot · Chongqing Xiaomian · Chili Chicken · Hot and Sour Noodles
07 · Guangdong

Chaozhou

Pure flavors and aromatic braises meet rice-cake craft and glowing charcoal.

Fresh Beef Hot Pot · Oyster Omelet · Master-Stock Goose · Red Peach Rice Cake
08 · Guangxi

Liuzhou

Sour, hot, savory, and steaming flavors converge in one audacious bowl of noodles.

Luosifen · Sanjiang Oil Tea · Stuffed Tofu · Bamboo Rice
09 · Jilin

Yanji

Icy noodles and glowing charcoal bring Korean-Chinese traditions together on the border.

Yanji Cold Noodles · Rice Sausage · Charcoal Skewers · Stone-Pot Bibimbap
10 · Zhejiang

Taizhou

Between mountain and sea, chewy rice snacks share the table with vivid seafood.

Fanfenyuan · Braised Hairtail · Pan-Fried Yellow Croaker · Steamed Blue Crab
11 · Xinjiang

Kashgar

Tandoor smoke and warm spice meet the bright fruit of the western oases.

Lamb Polu · Tandoor Samsa · Xinjiang Naan · Lamb Skewers
12 · Jiangsu

Yangzhou

Knife work, clear broths, and gentle flavors unfold over an unhurried morning tea.

Braised Shredded Tofu · Three-Dice Bao · Red-Braised Lion's Head · Yangzhou Fried Rice
13 · Beijing

Beijing

Crisp duck skin, deep bean-paste savor, and an entire old-city repertoire hidden in the hutongs.

Peking Duck · Beijing Zhajiangmian · Douzhir · Copper-Pot Mutton
14 · Shanghai

Shanghai

Glossy soy braises meet intricate dumplings, gathering sweet, savory, and fresh flavors in every bite.

Xiaolongbao · Shengjian Mantou · Red-Braised Pork · Scallion-Oil Noodles
15 · Zhejiang

Hangzhou

Tea fragrance, lake fish, and gentle sweet-sour flavors turn Jiangnan scenery into a meal.

West Lake Fish in Vinegar Gravy · Dongpo Pork · Longjing Prawns · Shrimp and Eel Noodles
16 · Jiangsu

Nanjing

Duck runs through every meal, while soup dumplings and sweet rice desserts soften the ancient capital.

Nanjing Salted Duck · Duck Blood Vermicelli Soup · Chicken-Broth Soup Dumplings · Red Bean Jiuniang Rice Balls

Tianjin

Breakfast griddles ring out as jianbing, gabacai, and gravy noodles feed this candid port city.

Goubuli Baozi · Jianbing Guozi · Gabacai · Three-Delicacy Dalumian

Harbin

Sweet-sour pork, smoked sausage, and giant rye loaves supply serious warmth in the northern cold.

Guobaorou · Harbin Red Sausage · Dalieba Bread · Soy-Braised Pork Bones

Satay-peanut richness and sweet shellfish give Minnan snacks the scent of the sea.

Shacha Noodles · Tusundong · Oyster Omelet · Xiamen Fried Rice Vermicelli

Kunming

Flowers, rice noodles, and a steam pot make the Spring City taste fresh and wonderfully untamed.

Crossing-the-Bridge Rice Noodles · Steam-Pot Chicken · Grilled Erkuai · Yunnan Flower Cake

Guiyang

Fermented sour broth wakes the palate while toasted chilies build a deeper, fragrant heat.

Siwawa · Sour-Broth Beef · Douhua Noodles · Red-Braised Beef Rice Noodles

Lanzhou

Clear broth, white radish, red chili, green herbs: one noodle bowl wakes the northwest.

Lanzhou Beef Noodles · Niangpi · Hand-Grabbed Lamb · Lamb Vermicelli Soup
