BEYOND THE CLASSICS

Eleven hidden-gem food cities

The most memorable food trip is often one stop beyond the obvious itinerary. These cities reward travelers with distinctive local ingredients, techniques, and everyday eating rituals.
01 · Guangdong

Chaozhou

Pure flavors and aromatic braises meet rice-cake craft and glowing charcoal.

Fresh Beef Hot Pot · Oyster Omelet · Master-Stock Goose · Red Peach Rice Cake
02 · Guangxi

Liuzhou

Sour, hot, savory, and steaming flavors converge in one audacious bowl of noodles.

Luosifen · Sanjiang Oil Tea · Stuffed Tofu · Bamboo Rice
03 · Jilin

Yanji

Icy noodles and glowing charcoal bring Korean-Chinese traditions together on the border.

Yanji Cold Noodles · Rice Sausage · Charcoal Skewers · Stone-Pot Bibimbap
04 · Zhejiang

Taizhou

Between mountain and sea, chewy rice snacks share the table with vivid seafood.

Fanfenyuan · Braised Hairtail · Pan-Fried Yellow Croaker · Steamed Blue Crab
05 · Xinjiang

Kashgar

Tandoor smoke and warm spice meet the bright fruit of the western oases.

Lamb Polu · Tandoor Samsa · Xinjiang Naan · Lamb Skewers
06 · Jiangsu

Yangzhou

Knife work, clear broths, and gentle flavors unfold over an unhurried morning tea.

Braised Shredded Tofu · Three-Dice Bao · Red-Braised Lion's Head · Yangzhou Fried Rice
07 · Jiangsu

Nanjing

Duck runs through every meal, while soup dumplings and sweet rice desserts soften the ancient capital.

Nanjing Salted Duck · Duck Blood Vermicelli Soup · Chicken-Broth Soup Dumplings · Red Bean Jiuniang Rice Balls
08 · Heilongjiang

Harbin

Sweet-sour pork, smoked sausage, and giant rye loaves supply serious warmth in the northern cold.

Guobaorou · Harbin Red Sausage · Dalieba Bread · Soy-Braised Pork Bones
09 · Fujian

Xiamen

Satay-peanut richness and sweet shellfish give Minnan snacks the scent of the sea.

Shacha Noodles · Tusundong · Oyster Omelet · Xiamen Fried Rice Vermicelli
10 · Yunnan

Kunming

Flowers, rice noodles, and a steam pot make the Spring City taste fresh and wonderfully untamed.

Crossing-the-Bridge Rice Noodles · Steam-Pot Chicken · Grilled Erkuai · Yunnan Flower Cake
11 · Guizhou

Guiyang

Fermented sour broth wakes the palate while toasted chilies build a deeper, fragrant heat.

Siwawa · Sour-Broth Beef · Douhua Noodles · Red-Braised Beef Rice Noodles
