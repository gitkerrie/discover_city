## Reddit Post Draft

**Title:** 12 Chinese food cities beyond Beijing and Shanghai

China’s regional food changes dramatically from city to city. I put together a compact map covering six classics and six less obvious stops, with four signature dishes and a local eating tip for each city.

The classics are Chengdu, Xi’an, Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan, and Chongqing. The less obvious stops are Chaozhou, Liuzhou, Yanji, Taizhou, Kashgar, and Yangzhou.

If you prefer spice, start with Chengdu, Chongqing, or Changsha. For breakfast culture, try Guangzhou, Wuhan, or Yangzhou. For noodles, compare Xi’an, Liuzhou, and Yanji.
